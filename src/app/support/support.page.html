Frequently Asked Questions
How do I start a "Pint?" session?

Easy! From the main dashboard, tap the big "Fancy a Pint?" card. You'll then be shown a map of nearby pubs. Simply select your chosen pub, and we'll notify other users nearby.

How do notifications work?

When someone starts a session, we check if the pub they chose is within the notification radius you set in your preferences. If it is, you'll get a push notification. You can adjust this radius and the notification sound in Settings > Notification Preferences.

How do I edit my profile?

Navigate to your profile by tapping your avatar on the dashboard. From there, tap the pencil icon in the top-right corner to open the 'Edit Profile' screen where you can change your photo, name, and favourite tipple.
